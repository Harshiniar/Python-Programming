If the units are less than or equal to 400, the charge is Rs.0.65 per unit with an additional fixed charge of Rs.100. If the units are less than or equal to 600, the charge is Rs.0.80 per unit with an additional fixed charge of Rs.200. If the units consumed are more than 600, the charge is Rs.1.25 per unit with an additional fixed charge of Rs.425. The program should calculate and display the total electricity bill in rupees based on the units consumed.
